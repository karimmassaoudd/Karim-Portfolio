BRANDING

Result:

The final branding outcome delivers a complete digital identity tailored to Owen Bryce’s artistic persona. Through collaborative design and strategic planning, we created a coherent visual and emotional representation of his music and character. Deliverables include:
A fully envisioned website design that showcases Owen’s music, personality, and story A custom logo reflecting his love for freedom and the dreamy tone of his music A brand guide to maintain consistency in visuals and messaging A content strategy to help him grow his fanbase organically All elements reflect Owen’s essence as a free-spirited, optimistic dreamer.

×

ASSIGNMENT:

Our task was to develop a full branding package for Owen Bryce, an upcoming indie/pop and bedroom folk artist studying at the Rock Academy in Tilburg. Scope included:
Designing a personalized logo Building a music-centered website Creating a branding guide Developing a marketing and content strategy
This entire process focused on capturing his three key values: freedom, optimism, and dreaming.

CONTENT STRATEGIE:

The content strategy was designed to authentically communicate Owen’s personality while increasing reach and engagement. Key elements: Tone & Style: Emotional, reflective, and hopeful Visuals: Nature-inspired, soft tones, and atmospheric aesthetics Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube with music snippets, vlogs, and stories Fan Engagement: Newsletter signup, fan section, blog Future Integration: Merch store, performance calendar Inspiration: Artists like Noah Kahan, whose branding deeply connects with listeners through visuals and emotional storytelling.

feedback:

Owen’s feedback guided the project and helped refine the branding: He loved the dreamy visuals that matched his artistic identity He stressed the importance of reflecting his values across all media His responses helped improve the structure and emotional tone of the content This feedback made the branding feel deeply personal and aligned with his artistic goals.

×

PROJECT DESCRIPTION:

This project, completed by a six-person team using Agile methodology, aimed to turn Owen Bryce’s creative vision into a strong, digital brand. Included in the project: A website featuring his biography, latest music, video performances, social links, and merch A logo representing his unique music and energy A brand guide outlining tone, color palette, typography, and image style A content strategy tailored to his audience (ages 16–35) Owen’s indie/folk style inspired our use of calm, natural visuals and emotional storytelling.

RESEARCH:

Thorough research ensured that the branding was thoughtful and targeted. Our process included: Interviews with Owen to understand his personality, audience, and artistic direction Audience research focused on individuals aged 16–35 looking for meaning and identity Genre analysis to understand trends in indie/folk music branding Visual inspiration from artists like Noah Kahan Psychological research into colors and imagery that reflect freedom, nature, and emotion This research helped us craft a brand that is relatable, expressive, and built to grow.
